How it works

  1. Import a photo of a drawing, or pick a sample.
  2. Slide Line strength until the drawing looks clean, then tap Color this.
  3. Pick a color and a tool, then color inside the lines.
  4. Tap ☰ → Save to keep it in your gallery.
