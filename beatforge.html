Song mode

A pattern is one loop. A song plays your patterns in order — like intro → verse → chorus. Capture your current beat as a pattern, then tap patterns to build a timeline.

Step 2 · Your song
Tap a pattern above to add it to the timeline. Use − and ＋ to set how many bars each one plays.

Add your own sounds

Turn any audio into a pad you can play in time with the rest of your track. Two ways:

Each sound you add gets a type you choose (that sets its icon and color so your library stays sorted), is saved inside the app so it’s here next time, and plays through your FX and the recorder like any other pad. It shows up as a new pad in every kit and in ★ My Board. Short, punchy sounds work best — recordings are trimmed for you.

✂ Trim audio

Drag the two green handles to pick the part you want to keep. Press ▶ to hear just that piece on a loop, then save it as a pad you can play.
