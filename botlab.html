👪 BreedingHealthy, grown foragers have young that inherit their brain.
☠️ Survival of the fittestStarving means death, not a nap. Turns the world into real natural selection.
🎨 Heritable traitsEach creature's colour shows its strengths. 🔴 nose · 🟢 thrift · 🔵 belly. Traits are a fixed budget, so nobody's good at everything.
🌦️ WeatherSeasons that shift on their own: 🌧️ rain (flowers thrive), 🔥 heat & ❄️ cold (burn faster), 🌫️ fog (can't smell far).
🦊 PredatorsHunters that chase the colony. Foragers can learn to flee and hide behind walls.
🐰 Prey to huntLittle critters that run. One creature can't catch them alone, so the group has to team up. The catch is shared.
🗺️ World sizeA bigger world gives tribes room to spread out and hold real territory. Changing size starts a fresh world.
🛖 TribesFamilies stick together when fed and form home turf. Predators pick off loners, so grouping keeps them safer. Each tribe has its own color and badge.
⏳ AgingCreatures grow old and pass on, not just starve. A life has an arc.
👫 Two parentsNeeds a male + female side by side to make a baby. The baby takes a random share of each parent's traits and brain. Off = a lone adult clones itself.
